Resources

Welcome to our curated list of resources for rock music enthusiasts! Whether you're a seasoned rocker or just starting your journey into the world of rock, these recommendations are sure to enhance your appreciation for the genre.

The Beatles - Abbey Road Led Zeppelin - IV Pink Floyd - The Dark Side of the Moon Nirvana - Nevermind The Rolling Stones - Exile on Main St. Queen - A Night at the Opera AC/DC - Back in Black Bruce Springsteen - Born to Run Radiohead - OK Computer The Who - Who's Next

Hammer of the Gods: The Led Zeppelin Saga" by Stephen Davis "Please Kill Me: The Uncensored Oral History of Punk" by Legs McNeil and Gillian McCain "Life" by Keith Richards "Just Kids" by Patti Smith "Chronicles: Volume One" by Bob Dylan "Our Band Could Be Your Life: Scenes from the American Indie Underground, 1981-1991" by Michael Azerrad "Meet Me in the Bathroom: Rebirth and Rock and Roll in New York City 2001-2011" by Lizzy Goodman "Scar Tissue" by Anthony Kiedis
